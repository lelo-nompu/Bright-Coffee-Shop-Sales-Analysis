BRIGHT COFFEE SHOP SALES ANALYSIS

A.	Project Overview

This project analyses transactional sales data from Bright Coffee Shop to uncover key business insights that can support strategic decision-making. The analysis was conducted in response to a new CEO’s objective to increase revenue and improve product performance.

The dataset contains detailed records of daily transactions, including product information, quantities sold, pricing, store locations, and timestamps.

B.	Objectives

The main objectives of this analysis were to:

1.	Identify which products generate the most revenue;
2.	Determine peak sales times during the day;
3.	Analyse sales trends over time;
4.	Evaluate store performance; and
5.	Provide actionable recommendations to improve business performance.

D.	Methodology

1.	Data Preparation

1.1.	Converted raw Excel dataset into CSV format
1.2.	Uploaded data into Databricks
1.3.	Cleaned and transformed data
1.3.1.	Standardized pricing format
1.3.2.	Created calculated column: `revenue = transaction_qty × unit_price`
1.3.3.	Created time categories (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night)

2.	Data Analysis (SQL)

2.1.	Aggregated revenue by product type
2.2.	Analysed sales by time of day and day of week
2.3.	Calculated total revenue and units sold
2.4.	Compared performance across store locations
2.5.	Identified high-performing vs low-performing products

3.	Data Visualisation

3.1.	Created pivot tables in Excel to explore patterns
3.2.	Built an interactive dashboard in Power BI including
3.2.1.	Revenue by product type
3.2.2.	Sales by time of day
3.2.3.	Daily sales trends
3.2.4.	Store performance
3.2.5.	KPI metrics (Total Revenue & Units Sold)

E.	Key Insights

1.	Certain product types contribute significantly more to total revenue;
2.	Sales peak during specific time periods, indicating optimal business hours;
3.	Some products have high sales volume but lower revenue contribution;
4.	Store performance varies, highlighting opportunities for optimisation; and
5.	Sales trends fluctuate over time, indicating demand patterns.

F.	Recommendations

1.	Increase inventory and promotion of top-performing products;
2.	Introduce targeted marketing during low-sales periods;
3.	Reassess pricing strategies for low-revenue products;
4.	Optimise staffing during peak hours; and
5.	Focus on improving performance in lower-performing store locations.

G.	Conclusion

This analysis provides a data-driven foundation for improving operational efficiency and increasing revenue at Bright Coffee Shop. By leveraging insights from sales trends, product performance, and customer behaviour, the business can make informed strategic decisions.
